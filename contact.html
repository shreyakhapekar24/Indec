FAQ

In what style of décor do you specialize?

We interpret the personalities and decor tastes of our clients into living spaces that are refined, modern, luxurious, exotic and comfortable.

How long does the entire design process take?

Generally, six months is a good rule of thumb. Smaller projects can take as little as three months. Larger projects can take upwards of one year.

How do I choose the right designer for me?

The collaboration between you and your interior designer is very personal. The first thing you should consider is whether you like the designer as a person and what they stand for.

How much will it cost?

Costs vary depending on the scope of projects. There's a range depending the scope of the project. This is determined through the estimate we produce after the consultation in which we spell out a high to low range.

Why should I hire an interior designer?

Interior Designers not only have a keen eye for detail, knowing what works and what doesn’t work together, but they are also experience trouble shooters.

What does an interior designer do?

Interior Designers turn visions into reality. We work from the inside out, taking into consideration the architectural style, geographical location, lifestyle and preference of our clients, as well as any other parameters.
